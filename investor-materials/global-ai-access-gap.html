TAM: Bridging the Global AI Access Gap

The AI industry has a scale problem. While almost a billion people are already using AI, most platforms are still architected for the 1%: users with high-end hardware and unlimited data. In emerging markets, millions of data-poor smartphone users are still blocked from the true power of intelligence.
