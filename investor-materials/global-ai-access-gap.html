Global AI Access Gap

The AI industry has a scale problem. This note explains why the next wave of AI growth depends on serving data-poor smartphone users, and how AI-for-All uses edge-cloud orchestration to make that economically viable.

The problem

AI platforms are still largely designed for users with premium devices, strong connectivity and the ability to absorb high inference costs.

The insight

Millions of users in emerging markets own smartphones with enough hardware capacity, but remain excluded by data cost and cloud-heavy AI economics.

The solution

  • Route a large share of low-token queries on-device.
  • Use cloud only when complexity truly requires it.
  • Reduce cost by roughly 50-80% for scalable access.
